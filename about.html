About Empirical Co

Strategic consulting that drives real results

Who We Are

Empirical Co is a strategic consulting firm that partners with domestic and international clients to deliver transformative business solutions. We bridge the gap between vision and execution, combining deep industry expertise with a hands-on approach to solve complex challenges.

From technology implementation to supply chain optimization, from media campaigns to market entry strategy, we bring senior-level strategic thinking to businesses of all sizes. Our team has worked across diverse sectors and geographies, giving us unique insights into what drives success in today's interconnected marketplace.

What We Do

We deliver end-to-end solutions across three core pillars:

Technology & Operations: From SaaS procurement and technical solution design to agentic AI implementation, we help businesses leverage technology to drive efficiency and growth.

Supply Chain & Commerce: Specializing in cross-border e-commerce, supply chain strategy, compliance, and international partnerships, we navigate the complexities of global trade.

Communications & Brand: Through our Little Bike Communications division, we craft compelling brand stories and execute integrated campaigns that connect with audiences.
